Third‑Party Services
CATSUP and RELISH – Anthropic Claude API
CATSUP and RELISH use Anthropic's Claude API (api.anthropic.com) to generate
responses to what you type.
Your typed questions or messages and conversation context are sent in real time to
Anthropic:
CATSUP: Socratic questions only (never direct answers)
RELISH: Compressed emotional clarity (max 3 sentences)

We retain this data only for the current session unless you enable local
progress/history, which is stored only on your device.
We share only the minimum data required to provide responses. We do not give
Anthropic permission to use your data to train their models. However, Anthropic's own
terms and privacy practices apply to their systems; please review them for details.
Anthropic privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
By using the chat features in CATSUP or RELISH, you consent to this limited sharing.
BBQE – RapidAPI Breach Checks (Pit Boss tier only)
BBQE uses RapidAPI to perform breach checks for the Pit Boss tier.
Your email address is sent to breachdirectory.p.rapidapi.com only when you explicitly
request a breach check.
We do not store your email for any other purpose.
RapidAPI privacy policy: https://rapidapi.com/legal/privacy
By using the breach checker, you consent to this limited sharing.

Data Storage & Security
Most data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.
When cloud storage or processing is required, we use encrypted servers (AES‑256 at
rest, TLS in transit).
Access to any backend data is restricted to the 3.6.NIFE engineering team on a
need‑to‑know basis.
If you request deletion of data that we control on our servers, we delete it within 30
days.

Breach Notification
If we ever experience a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify
affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, via in‑app notification
and/or the email address associated with your account. We will describe what
happened, what data was affected, and what steps we are taking to resolve it.
Children's Privacy
Our apps are rated 4+ and are designed with children's privacy in mind. We follow
Apple's child‑directed app rules:
We minimize data collection from children.
We do not use data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or cross‑app tracking.
If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child beyond what is
necessary to operate the app, we will delete it as quickly as possible.
